# Local Node Shop

Any time the party enters their Local Node (as long as some webrunning or out-of-web adventuring has been done) the Local Node Shop will refresh. 

The shop uses the highest Luck stat of anybody in the party to generate as many pieces of gear as there are members of the party, which can then be bought with essence or credits.

| Rarity    | Essence Cost | Credit Cost |
| --------- | ------------ | ----------- |
| Common    | 500          | 50          |
| Uncommon  | 1000         | 100         |
| Rare      | 2000         | 200         |
| Epic      | 5000         | 500         |
| Legendary | 10000        | 1000        |

Essence and Credits can be transferred back and forth at a 10:1 ratio at the Local Node Shop.

Each of these upgrades are purchased *separately!* For example, you can have a shop that's at Tier I Shop Price and Tier III Shop Gear Amount Offered.

| Tier II  | 2000                                 | Total 20% off original prices | +4             | Party Members + 2        | Total 30% off original refresh price | 8000                          |
| Tier III | 4000                                 | Total 30% off original prices | +6             | Party Members + 3        | Total 45% off original refresh price | 16000                         |

# Gear Upgrading

While in your Local Node you can choose to spend essence to upgrade existing gear!

Make sure you don't go over [[#Gear Maxes]] while upgrading.

Upgrading entails:
- Getting new modifiers:
	- Rolling for a new Color Stat Buff
	- Rolling for either a new Active or Passive
- Upgrading Stat Buffs by going to the [[#Stat Buff Table]] and moving the Stat Buff to the new rarity value on the chart (it will regenerate the stat based on your current Luck stat)
	- If the previous Stat Buff is higher than the upgraded one then take the higher number! Really only applicable if the piece of gear was initially generated with bonus Luck.
- Upgrading Actives and Passives by moving them to the next tier of rarity

| Upgrade            | Additional <br>Color Stat Buff | Additional <br>Actives or Passives | Essence Cost |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------- | ------------ |
| Common -> Uncommon | +1                             | -                                  | 1000         |
| Uncommon -> Rare   | +1                             | +1 Active                          | 2000         |
| Rare -> Epic       | +1                             | -                                  | 5000         |
| Epic -> Legendary  | +1                             | +1 Active                          | 10000        |

# Rerolling Gear Modifiers

At your Local Node you can choose to completely reroll the Stat Buffs, Actives, and Passives of an item you already own.

Make sure that the piece of gear ends up with as many Stat Buffs, Actives, and Passives as it had before the reroll. 

Rerolling uses whatever your Luck stat is when it's initiated, so your new stats will scale with Luck.

Also make sure that if you've condensed Stat Buffs and Actives into the piece of gear that what you reroll for those condensed modifiers are of the same color or category as the original modifiers (e.g. if you condense a Green Stat Buff into a Red piece of gear make sure that you reroll another Green Stat Buff to replace the original).

| Reroll    | Essence Cost |
| --------- | ------------ |
| Common    | 250          |
| Uncommon  | 500          |
| Rare      | 1000         |
| Epic      | 2500         |
| Legendary | 5000         |

# Gear Condensing

At your Local Node you can choose to destroy a piece of gear and move a portion of their Stat Buffs, Actives, and/or Passives to a new piece of gear.

Make sure you don't exceed the Gear Maxes below when condensing!

# Aesthetic Modification

At your Local Node you can spend a little bit of Essence to change or swap the aesthetics of a piece of gear.

| Modification           | Essence Cost | Details                                                             |
| ---------------------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Aesthetic Swap         | 100          | Swap the aesthetic type between any two pieces of gear.             |
| Aesthetic Modification | 300          | Modify the aesthetic of a piece of gear to look however you'd like. |

## Aesthetic Modification Upgrades

You can purchase permanent upgrades to your Local Node that allow you to change the aesthetic of your gear whenever you'd like (as long as you're at your home node).

| Upgrade              | Essence Cost | Description                                                                                                                                                                               |
| -------------------- | ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Mirror               | 10000        | Use the Mirror to change the appearance of your character (does not affect gear). Can be modified to become any sort of mirror-like item of any aesthatic.                                |
| Modification Station | 10000        | Use the Modification Station to change the appearance of any of your gear. Can be modified to look like anything, such as a changing room, workshop, big stationary magic wand, anything. |
